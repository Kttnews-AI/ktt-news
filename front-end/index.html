🎯 Our Mission

Centrinsic NPT is an AI-powered news aggregation platform that provides simplified summaries of news articles from publicly available and trusted sources. The app displays AI-powered news cards that present short, easy-to-read summaries to help users quickly understand the key points of a story without scrolling through long articles. We do not produce original journalism. All news content, headlines, images, and articles belong to their respective publishers. Our platform uses AI technology only to generate brief summaries of publicly available news content. Each news card includes the original source name and a link to the full article so users can access complete details directly from the publisher.
